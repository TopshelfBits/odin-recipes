Stardew Valley - Lobster Bisque

+=

Description

The Stardew Valley lobster bisque is a special dish, and unless you are close friends with Willy, it's quite difficult to get your hands on this secret recipe. Today I will be sharing this meal with all of you on behalf of Willy's permission. A delicious soup involving just a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 lobster
  • 1 milk (or large milk)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 4 closes garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Step by Step

  1. Use a large pot to fill with water half-way. Bring to a boil over high heat. As it begins boiling, add lobers for 8-10 minutes (or until shells are bright red). Save the water for your lobster base when finished.
  2. Cut through the shell with shears, or chop in half to pull out the flesh. Remove any veins, chopping into bite-size pieces. Throw the shells back into the water, and boil for another 10 minutes. After boiling, strain the water, saving 1 1/2 cups of the base.
  3. Heal olive oil in a sauce pan with medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add paprika, tomato paste and lobster base. Stir to combine well. Simmer while uncovered for ~10 minutes.
  5. Blend the mixture until very smooth in a separate blender. Return the mix back into the pan, and add the milk and lobster meat. Taste test, while adding your salt and pepper as needed. Heat through.
