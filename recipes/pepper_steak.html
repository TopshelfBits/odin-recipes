Zelda - Pepper Steak

Description

Hyrule is rich with a variety of foods ranging from fruits, vegetables, and meats. There is a meal to be had for any carnivore or vegetarian. With this pepper steak recipe, you will master one of the most popular dishes in the land.

Ingredients

  • Any type of Meat or Poultry
  • Any vegetable or mushroom
  • For cold resistance, include one or more spicy pepper

Steps

  1. Let the steak sit for about 20 minutes after hunting. (room temp) Meanwhile, prepare the topping(s) you desire. I prefer the mushrooms. Place them into aluminum foil. Heat gas grill to medium-high.
  2. Place mushrooms directly onto grill. Cover for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through and tender.
  3. Now remove the mushrooms, and increase the grill to high heat.Season the steak to your liking, I usually use salt and pepper. Grill for 4 to 5 minutes or slightly charred. Turn the steaks over for another 3-5 minutes (medium-rare), 5 to 7 minutes (medium), 8 to 10 (medium-well).
  4. Transfer steak to cutting board. Rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  5. Top with your peppers, mushrooms, etc, and enjoy.

Benefits

  • Increases heart health depending on the amount of meat used
  • If made with spicy peppers = cold resistance
